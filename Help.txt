For running animation:
1. Type  "  make '.RECIPEPREFIX+='  "
2. Then type ./main & press enter
3. Then type either "normal" or "exp-res" 
The various features of this animation are:
1. Press U for moving fractal upwards
2. Press D for moving fractal downwards
3. Press L for moving fractal left
4. Press R for moving fractal right
5. Press S to take screenshot of the fractal
6. See titlebar for zoom level
